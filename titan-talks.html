Titan Talks: Empowering Students Through Speaker Development

Titan Talks Program

Join us for a 5-week virtual training workshop to hone your public speaking skills and give you the opportunity to share your story and ideas to a live virtual audience! The workshop meets once a week on Saturdays from 1-2pm starting March 20th with the live performance on Saturday, April 24th starting at 1 pm. Members of TEDxCSUF who complete the series will be eligible to present.

Click Here to Sign Up!

Brainstorming 101

Getting Started – Saturday March 20th 1-2pm

Our first workshop will inspire you to select the story/topic you want to commit to. We suggest a 3-5-minute narrative speech to help you find your voice and get comfortable presenting.

Speech Writing

Getting Pen to Paper – Saturday March 27th 1-2pm

We will prepare you with the basics of speech composition and style notes so that you can begin your rough drafts with confidence!

A Delicate Balance

Refining Your Ideas - Saturday April 10th 1-2pm

In this workshop we will help you with the editing process. We will help you hone your message and focus on connecting with the audience.

Prepping for the Stage

Don’t Forget to Smile! - Saturday April 17th 1-2pm

Our final workshop will focus on what to expect when presenting and how to prepare. Our featured speaker will give you the tips on how to connect through the screen.

The Grand Show!

Saturday April 24th 1-2pm

This is the virtual presentation where everyone who completed the series will present their speech to a live audience!
